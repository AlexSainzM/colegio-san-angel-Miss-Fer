3Ô∏è‚É£ Spelling Rules for -ING

Rule Base Verb + ING
Most verbs: add -ING play, watch, read, listen playing, watching, reading, listening

Short verbs (CVC): double last consonant + ING run, swim, sit, shop running, swimming, sitting, shopping
Verbs ending in -IE: change IE to Y + ING lie, die lying, dying

8Ô∏è‚É£ Writing About Your Preferences

üìù Useful Connectors:

  • and - to add similar ideas
  • also - to add more information
  • but / however - to show contrast
  • because - to give a reason
  • especially - to emphasize
Example Paragraph:

"I love playing video games because they are exciting and fun. I also really enjoy listening to music, especially pop and rock. I like going to the cinema with my friends on weekends. However, I don't like waking up early on Saturdays. I hate doing homework on Sundays because I prefer relaxing. I don't mind walking to school because it's good exercise."
