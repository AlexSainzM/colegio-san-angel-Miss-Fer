üìå When do we use Present Perfect?

  • Experiences in life: I have visited Paris. (sometime in my life)
  • Recent actions with results now: I have lost my keys. (I can't find them now)
  • Actions that started in the past and continue now: I have lived here for 5 years.
  • With: just, already, yet, ever, never, for, since
